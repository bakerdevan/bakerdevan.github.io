About Us

Here at Evergreen Home and Deck Company, our mission is to provide the utmost excellence in Log Home Restoration, Home Remodeling, and Deck Services. When it comes to your home needs we are here for you! Personalized service is what we do. From start to finish, your input is valued so your satisfaction is 100% guaranteed! Experience the Evergreen Difference today!

I grew up on a small farm in Southeast Missouri, It was there I first learned carpentry and home remodeling. As a young man working side by side with my Father and Grandfather we built Decks, Pole Barns, Fences, Remodeled Kitchens, Bathrooms, Living Rooms, Home Exteriors, Painting, and Staining. The lessons and values I learned have stuck with me throughout my career. Shortly after college, I started working in the Carpenters Union in St. Louis, Missouri. Over the next five years, I learned my trade inside and out and progressed quite quickly to become a Foreman running my own crew of guys. It was challenging at times but my patience and determination always got me through. Shortly after leaving the Carpenters Union I took a job at a Log Home Restoration Co. Starting out as a Laborer / Carpenter I worked my way up through the ranks and before I knew it I was a Project Manager in charge of Field Operations. Working side by side with the crew to ensure the project was completed on time and our customer's expectations were met. My upfront attitude and strong work ethics have made me a Reliable, Trustworthy, & Dependable. I want to share the passion I have for the work we do with and for our Customers. My goal is to EXCEED your expectations in every aspect of the work we do. We look forward to working with you & building a long-term relationship.

Yours Truly,

Dan Stricklan

Owner, CEO
